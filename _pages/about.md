Shuojun Li received his B.S. degree in Chemistry from Huazhong Agricultural University in 2017 and his Ph.D. in Chemical Biology in 2023. He then joined the Center for Excellence in Molecular Cell Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, as a postdoctoral researcher. In 2025, he was appointed as an Assistant Professor at the School of Life Sciences, Fudan University. His research focuses on bridging chemistry and biology to pioneer new research fields. He has published multiple papers in journals such as Nature and Angewandte, with his work highlighted by Nature Chemical Biology, Cancer Discovery, and other international media outlets.
